About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at Oklahoma Christian University specializing in declarative programming and Explainable AI. My research encompasses both theoretical foundations and practical implementations in these fields, with applications in Computer Aided Design (CAD), cybersecurity, and assistive technologies.
